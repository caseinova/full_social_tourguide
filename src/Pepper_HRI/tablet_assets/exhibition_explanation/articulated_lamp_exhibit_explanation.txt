This last exhibition is a culmination of many of the aspects from previous exhibitions and also symbolises my purpose in this tour:

The efficient LEDs are placed in a ring to disperse light and illuminate whatever it might be pointed at so that you humans might see the subject with more clarity.

While the joints are not freely moving, the 9 total joints create an exponentially large amount of positions which the end affector may exist in, allowing for incredible versatility in movement and function in it's respective purpose.

With a combination of cams, springs, and pulleys, this mechanism has been perfectly balanced such that the position of the end affector follows the exact kinematic instruction of the human user, and also could be considered to remember instructions and maintain useful configurations.


This is the articulated desk lamp, and is a combination of many of the scientific concepts of the other available exhibits.
