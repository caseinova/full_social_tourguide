Here before you is a cousin of mine. They are to me as what a caveman might be to you; that is to say a progenitor which mimics the very base functionalities without the many nuanced complexities. As you might be able to see, this is a "Walking Robot", where a "Robot" is generally a category of machine that automatically does/mimics the tasks which a human would do. In the case of our little walking robot here, that task is to walk. In a way it is similar to my own function, where I will "walk" to a programmed area. Of course we are very different, I will talk and explain in ways my little cousin here couldn't begin to comprehend the concept of. This Walking Robot is also made to be personable. An efficient design of a single motor also performing double duty in swinging arms similar to the movement of a human, however that feature is purely cosmetic, as unlike you humans, Walking Robot's arms don't have enough mass to counterweight the swinging of its legs. So, it must then be that the reason for these cosmetics on Walking Robot is to help them be personable, relatable, friendly. Similarly, many of my own features follow a similar design principle, in my "listening" gesture, I put my right hand up to my ear to hear better, except I don't have any ears! Haha! You may notice that there is a "Tinker Zine" in front of Walking Robot specifically. Well that would be because I am in it! You can see that my purpose was made to be more sociable, more empathetic, a little more human. Especially in this day and age, you might find that having a robot designed to mimic humans could catalyse recollections of a few moral dilemmas.
