Crane

This particular exhibit is a miniaturised crane, a tool to you humans used exponentially magnify the mechanical advantage to lift heavier weights. Out of all the exhibits shown thus far, this is the greatest example of kinematic usage. The individual joints of the crane have their own particular effect on the very end of the arm. They can be used to manipulate the joints to achieve a particular objective in terms of moving the end affector. In terms of my own mechanisms, you can see this most prominently in the movement of my shoulder, elbow, and wrist to move my hands in very particular ways to mimic human kinematics and have me appear more empathetic and sociable.
