replace 192.168.1.50 with pepper IP


    Direct the Audio Output Stream over the Network:
pactl load-module module-tunnel-sink server=192.168.1.50

    Launch the ROS 2 Audio Capture Node:
ros2 run audio_capture audio_capture_node --ros-args -p dst:=192.168.1.50

    Open PulseAudio Network Ports:
pactl load-module module-native-protocol-tcp auth-anonymous=1

    Verify the Topic Connection:
ros2 topic hz /audio/audio
